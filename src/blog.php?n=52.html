Tuesday, 20th February || All decorated out

Well the week is up and I'm all decorated out... I've come back to work for a rest! Thanks to Mum & Dad we got loads done last week: we stripped, re-papered and painted the third bedroom, stripped and painted the downstairs WC, painted the upstairs WC, re-sealed the shower and the bath and got halfway through insulating the loft. Not bad for a week's work! Oh and we got someone round to clean the gutters, and I got a quote for double glazing. A very productive week I feel. Thanks to Mum for all her hard work and unlimited energy, and to Dad too for his hard work and attention to detail I couldn't have done it without you!

So I no longer have an orange WC, it's now minty green. But don't despair, all the orange accessories are now in the 3rd bedroom along with the new yellow walls. Waste not want not!

Now I've got to summon up the energy for Saturday's Middlesex Orbital badminton tournament. Thanks to Heather I've entered the Singles (!) and the Ladies doubles. We got to the Final of the latter last time, let's hope we can win it this time! That might be wishful thinking... I'm not holding out much hope for the singles though, I've never played singles competitively before, let alone in a national event Wish me luck!
