Tuesday, 19th June || Je retourne

I'm back from France after a fabulous weekend at La Leu, Helen's parents' place half an hour south of Poitiers celebrating Helen & Linden's engagement. Lots of chatting, lots of drinking and lots of fun was the name of the game

Petanque was the main entertainment, closely followed by frisbee and poker/cards on wet Sunday (and Dave when he'd had a few French lagers). I didn't do or see an awful lot, as I had too many people to catch up with, but I managed to squeeze in a couple of dips in the pool, a few games of frisbee, darts, table tennis and lots of eating and drinking.

Thanks to Linden and Helen for hosting the weekend and making us all feel welcome and comfortable. Photos can be viewed on Facebook here.
