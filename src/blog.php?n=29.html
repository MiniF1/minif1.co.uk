Thursday, 26th May || Result!

Well folks, good news - I exchanged contracts on my house today! At long last...

Completion day is on Tuesday, the last day of the month - talk about cutting it fine! Kerry very kindly offered to let me stay on the sofa if it didn't go through for Tuesday, but it seems I'll be sleeping on the floor in an empty house instead! Perhaps my first purchase should be a bed...

Cor blimey, that was a long, involved process. It'll be pretty much 12 weeks by the time I move in - and my estate agent reckoned it could happen in 6 weeks being as I had no chain! How wrong... But hey, we got there. Well, we will on Tuesday at least, fingers crossed!

And just in time for the BBQ season too, fantastic
