Wednesday, 1st June || I'm in!

Guess what - I'm a homeowner! I am officially in grown-up territory now I got the keys and moved in yesterday, and spent the first night in my very own home last night! I love it, it's fantastic I have a garden, a shed, a loft, a drive, and a hefty debt (but I'm glossing over that for the time being) and it's great! I totally recommend it.

I'm very lucky, as the house was let out before it was put up for sale, and a lot of stuff was therefore left behind, including a fridge, freezer, cooker (gas, hoorah!), washing machine, ironing board, curtains, lights, lampshades (although most of those will be going as soon as I can replace them, especially the frilly flowery ones!), washing line, BBQ, satellite dish... However, I am in need of a bed and a sofa to make it really habitable. Once I've got those, I think I'll really be settled in.

So anyone who's around the Southampton area at any time, please pop in and say hi! I have a couple of spare mugs and a working (if old and decrepit) kettle, so I can provide cups of tea and coffee; although if there's more than two of you you might have to bring your own chairs... I have two garden chairs and a bean bag to sit on at the moment!
