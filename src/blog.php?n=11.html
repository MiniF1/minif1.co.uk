What's going on in Caroline's world...

Tuesday, 21st September || Busy busy busy

Wow I have been busy recently. A weekend in London visiting lots of old friends I haven't seen for a while, traumas trying to replace the rear brakes on my car and a day driving my Dad's Porsche and my uncle's Renault Clio 172 around Castle Combe racing circuit. Then 4 days at work...

Then Grandma's 80th birthday party, which to me was a reunion of cousins - the first time all 10 of us were in one place in quite a few years, which was a good opportunity to catch up. Then three days at work...

Then last thursday I was off to Wales for the annual trip to the British round of the World Rally Championships (moved forward to September this year from November). This year though I was on my own, as so many of my fellow marshalls from the CSMA North London Marshall Team had boycotted the event for various reasons. Once I got past the traumas of thursday night and trying to find where I was supposed to be and the people I was working with, it all went smoothly. Friday was absolutely knackering but by far the best day, mingling with the co-drivers and organising everyone (I think I get my bossy streak from my Mum!). Saturday & Sunday at Margam were a doddle after friday, then it was home and back to work...

And what's in store this weekend? Hopefully a day trip to Chessington World of Adventures with Maz & Liz from work. Blimey it's been a few years since I've been there, but it's always a good laugh!

Ooh, and here's some exciting news - Gemma & Dan are engaged! How excited am I! Even though we saw it coming for ages They make such a lovely couple, I wish them both all the best.

That's it from me for now. I really ought to get some more photos up here though didn't I. Ok, I'll work on it.
