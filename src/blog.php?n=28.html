What's going on in Caroline's world...

Tuesday, 17th May || Snail's pace

I'm finding the house-buying process incredibly slow... I've signed a contract, got the mortgage offer, got the deposit ready, basically got everything in place that I can. I spoke to my solicitors last week, and we're now waiting to hear back from the seller with answers to a list of questions we put to him. Once we've got satisfactory answers (which very rarely happens first time apparently), we can then set dates for exchange etc.

It's been 8 weeks already, and I'm getting very nervous as I've got to be out of my flat by the end of May! Fingers crossed it will all go ok....!
