What's going on in Caroline's world...

Monday, 25th October || Back on form

I played my first badminton match for Wildern BC tonight, against Chandlers Ford. Carlos & I won 15-4, 15-13, then Janet and I took our Ladies game 15-3, 15-6. I thought they said this league would be tough!! I don't think the opposition had their strongest team out to be fair, but still!

It's a shame I can't make friday night's match, but then it's not every day you get the chance to see Bryan Adams live. Ok, so he's not the hippest of artists, and is getting on a bit, but I did grow up listening to him (that was the summer of '69!) and seeing anyone playing live is a treat - come on guys, surely it's better than Take That & Boyzone, the only other concerts I've been to!

Then I've got Gemma & Dan's Hallowe'en/Engagement party to look forward to on Saturday which should be a good laugh! And rumour has it, Heather's going to be there - a rare treat! Especially to have all three of us girlies in the same place at the same time (first time since we left uni I think!). I can't wait

And then I guess I'd better start thinking about packing... Kerry & I have got a new flat, hoorah! Our current landlord decided he wanted to sell our place, so we (well, mostly Kerry) went hunting and stumbled across a brand new development of flats over the road, so we snapped up the 2-bed penthouse suite It's not even finished yet! They do keep assuring us it'll be ready for the 1st, when we move in... I want to get a sofabed bed in it so you guys can all come and visit and have somewhere to stay. No excuses now!
