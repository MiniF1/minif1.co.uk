Monday, 13th March || New Car!

After 5 years of loyal service from my little red Saxo, I've finally traded it in. I now have an awesome Renault Clio 172 which is just too much fun It's the Cup version which is designed for track days etc, so a lot of the frills (e.g. Air Con, the spare wheel!) are stripped out to make it lighter. But even with three people in it goes like stink! I can't wait to get it to Castle Combe to really see what it can do, and to compare it to my uncle's standard 172
