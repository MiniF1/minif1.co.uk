Tuesday, 29th March || House

There's not much to report at the moment regarding the house. That's the scary thing about these things; once you've said you want a house and have agreed a price it's out of your control. You're at the mercy of solicitors and mortgage lenders and can only keep your fingers crossed that it all goes ok! Which is pretty much the stage I'm at now.

I sent off all the solicitor's forms last week, which hopefully they should have received now the Bank Holidays have passed. My mortgage advisor applied for my mortgage last week too, so the next stage there should be a survey/valuation by the mortgage company to verify that the house is worth what I've offered to pay for it and that it's not likely to fall down in the near future - I hope it passes muster

I'll post any updates here as soon as anything happens, so watch this space.

It does get frustrating though, trying not to get too excited about the house in case it all falls through. I've already mentally designed the new kitchen that I'd like to put in and decided how I'd like each room to look... I can't help myself! So any fingers and toes that you could keep crossed for me would be gratefully appreciated
