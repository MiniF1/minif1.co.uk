Thursday, May 08, 13:30 (Chile: BST -5:00) - Day 229
Back in Chile (briefly) - Atacama Desert
We spent a day in Tucuman, another in Salta and then Jujuy before heading up into the mountains to Tilcara (2400m), with its ruins of a pre-Inca fort and beautifully coloured mountains.

The journey back into Chile involved traversing the Andes, reaching altitudes of >4000m and crossing large bleak desert plains, ending in the oasis town of San Pedro de Atacama in the heart of the Atacama Desert. Here we hired bikes to explore the surrounding area and get to grips with the local culture and history, spending the evening watching the sun go down from the Valle de la Luna (Moon Valley - so-called because it resembles the surface of the moon) and cycling back in the dark (the stars were amazing) along dirt tracks.
