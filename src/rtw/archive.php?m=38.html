Monday, November 04, 17:50 (Hong Kong: BST +7:00) - Day 44
Hong Kong (might as well be Britain!)
After a night spent on the floor of Bangkok airport we arrived in Hong Kong last thursday. It's almost as much of a culture shock arriving here from Nepal as it was arriving in India - it's so civilised! And expensive to boot... We finally feel like travellers as we are staying in a hostel as opposed to a hotel, which has the benefit of a good location, a kitchen and lots of other travellers to talk to (and steal guide books from!).

We are only here for 6 days, but have managed to do quite a lot in a short time. So far we have visited the Peak with spectacular views over the skyscrapers and the harbour, walked round lots of street markets including birds, fish, flowers, clothes & handicrafts, had a boat tour round Aberdeen (no, not in Scotland) harbour and peered through the windows of lots of expensive shops! In terms of food we seem to be surviving by pointing at pictures to order, as the squiggly writing means absolutely nothing to us!

China is our next stop, so we look forward to reporting on that soon.
