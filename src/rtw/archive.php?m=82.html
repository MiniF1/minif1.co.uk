The End
The last sets of photos are back and will soon be up for all to see. The sharp ones amongst you may notice that the sequence jumps from the Colca Canyon to Cusco - unfortunately we lost the film containing the rest of Peru when our bag was stolen I hope you enjoy them.

I think that about rounds off our Round the World trip. We've been back now over 1.5 months and are settling back into life, with memories of scuba diving, eating piranha, climbing mountains and speaking something closely resembling Spanish slowly receding into the past...

We hope you've all enjoyed following our trip and that we haven't made you too green with envy. All that's left to say is thanks for all your e-mails while we were away and if you thought it looked fun, then why don't you go and do it too! We definitely recommend it
