South America - Chile / South Argentina
We arrived in Santiago [1] and sampled the South American culture, before heading south to Puerto Varas and Puerto Montt. We then got on a boat to Puerto Natales [2], and explored the wonderful scenery, before hopping on another boat and heading into Argentina and to Ushuaia.
