Monday, November 18, 09:40 (Vietnam: BST +6:00) - Day 58
North Vietnam
Since Yangshuo, we spent 1.5days travelling to Hanoi (via the Friendship Pass border) on various buses/minibuses - all fairly hassle free despite swapping buses at 3 in the morning! We met 2 English girls who had just graduated from Bath Uni and we used ecomonies of scale to barter down a room for all of us! We spent the first day wandering round the city and visiting the Army Museum then the next day we headed off to visit the Perfume Pagoda (a pagoda in a cave up a hill that we got to by bus/boat). That evening we jumped on a sleeper train to Lao Cai where we caught a bus (after many arguments about the price!) to the mountain Village of Sapa. We spent two days there wandering round the market where loads of minority people meet to sell their goods (all dressed up in their traditional costumes) and trekking down the valley to see some of their villages (Cat Cat, Lao Chai and Ta Van). Fortunately we managed to hitch a lift back to Sapa on the back of two motorbikes - a memorable (bumpy) experience!
