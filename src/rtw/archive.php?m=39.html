Monday, November 11, 14:15 (China: BST +7:00) - Day 51
Into China
We are now in a small but very touristy town called Yangshuo (near Guillin) on the river LiJiang. The bus here was very luxurious, plenty of legroom and they came round with water, soup and blankets! Yesterday we hired bikes and cycled round the amazing scenery with its rounded pillars just like the paintings you see of China - got to be seen to be believed! Have also taken a trip down the river to see all the wierd and wonderfully named rocks/hills (such as "nine horse fresco hill", "lion watching the nine horses" and "8 supernaturals crossing the river").

Tomorrow we start the journey to Vietnam (via Nanning) from where we have decided to go through Cambodia rather than Loas to get to Thailand.

Hope everyone enjoyed bonfire night!
