Tuesday, May 20, 17:30 (Peru: BST -6:00) - Day 241
Arequipa, Peru
Having finally done North Chile we have made it safely into Peru.

We haven't been up to mach since we last wrote - just a day trip to Lauca NP where we saw petroglyphs, old churches, guanacos, vizcachas (long tailed rabbits), andean gerbils (?), more flamingoes, lots of volcanoes and the highest Lake in the world (4515m) in a very exhausting day!

We left Arica yesterday morning in the capable hands of 3 chilean people on holiday who got us across the border to Tacna from where we caught a bus (7hrs) to Arequipa, Peru's 2nd largest city.

We have spent the day looking at churches and a monastry (actually a convent) and shopping around for tours to the nearby Colca Valley. We have just signed up for a 4 day trip to see Condors, do some trekking and get an intro to Andean Peru (training for the Inca trail).
