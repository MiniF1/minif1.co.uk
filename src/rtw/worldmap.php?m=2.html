India & Nepal
We flew into Delhi [1] on the 22nd September (very late in the evening mind!), and have now been through Jaipur (25th), Mandawa, Bikaner, Jaisalmer [2], Jodphur (1st Oct), Ranakpur, Udaipur [3], Pushkar, Agra (7th), Varanasi, and finally into Kathmandu [4] (12th).

Then we\'ve been white-water rafting for 9 days on the Sun Kosi [5] (look closely, just east of Kathmandu!), and trekking for 4 days near Pokhara [6]. We returned to Kathmandu for our flight out, on the 31st October.
