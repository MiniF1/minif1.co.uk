Wednesday, April 02, 20:00 (Chile: BST -5:00) - Day 193
Auckland to Santiago - the longest day of our lives!
It has been a very long day as our flight crossed the International Date Line and wednesday seems to be going on forever! In one day we have managed to spend the morning visiting the Maritime Museum in Auckland, sell off our camping gear (which went towards the departure tax they sprung on us at the airport), jump on a plane at 5pm, watch the new Harry Potter film, land at 12pm that same day (after a 10.5 hour flight - time travel!), then spend the afternoon touring Santiago and visiting a museum while trying to stay awake! Phew, we're knackered.
