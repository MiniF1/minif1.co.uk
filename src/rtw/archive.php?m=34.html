Tuesday, October 01, 19:51 (India: BST +4:30) - Day 10
Sunrise in the desert
Having been here just over a week now, we have managed to see quite a lot! We left Smelly Delhi after 3 days there, and were glad to see the back of the oppressing heat caused mainly by the pollution.

Since then we have been to Jaipur, where we rode an elephant up a hill, Mandawa with wall paintings & dodgy electricity, Bikaner with a nice fort, Jaisalmer in the middle of the desert where we rode camels and watched the sun set and rise over the dunes, and now we are in Jodphur.
